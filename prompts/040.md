PROMPT 036A

Enhance the existing product pricing system to support both Online Retail and Wholesale pricing
without splitting the product catalog.

IMPORTANT RULES

Do NOT change the architecture.
Do NOT create new product tables.
Do NOT split products into wholesale and online tables.

Extend the existing product_prices table.

--------------------------------------------------

GOAL

Allow a single product to support:

Retail pricing
Wholesale pricing
Bulk quantity pricing
Vendor-specific pricing
Seasonal offers

--------------------------------------------------

DATABASE UPDATE

Update table:

product_prices

Add the following columns:

price_type
sales_channel
min_quantity
start_date
end_date

--------------------------------------------------

COLUMN DETAILS

price_type

Possible values:

Retail
Wholesale
Vendor
Offer

--------------------------------------------------

sales_channel

Possible values:

Online
Wholesale
Vendor
Marketplace

--------------------------------------------------

min_quantity

Used for wholesale and bulk pricing.

Example

Retail price
min_quantity = 1

Wholesale price
min_quantity = 50

--------------------------------------------------

FINAL STRUCTURE

product_prices

id
product_id
product_variant_id
currency_id
price
price_type
sales_channel
min_quantity
start_date
end_date
created_at

--------------------------------------------------

SERVICE CHANGES

Update:

ProductService
SalesService

Price resolution must follow this order:

1 Check Offer price within date range
2 Check Wholesale price if quantity >= min_quantity
3 Check Vendor price if vendor context exists
4 Default to Retail price

--------------------------------------------------

FRONTEND

Update ProductForm.

Allow entering multiple price rows.

Fields:

price_type
sales_channel
min_quantity
price
start_date
end_date

--------------------------------------------------

VALIDATION

Ensure:

min_quantity >= 1

Retail price must always exist.

--------------------------------------------------

DO NOT MODIFY

products
product_variants
product_inventory
product_vendor_links
